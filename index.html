Background

By 1965, the Beatles had established themselves as a top pop/rock group around the world: it was during this time that they began to explore different styles of music, experimented with different recording techniques and more complex songs. They also experimented with drugs and began to grow weary of touring and publicity appearances as Beatlemania spiraled out of control. They began express their philosophical and political views, which sometimes proved controversial. The Beatles were seeking to shed their "Fab Four" image and had begun the transformation, rebranding themselves as icons of the psychedelic age, culminating in the release of the album "Sgt Pepper's Lonely Heart's Club Band" a year later.

Events

  • 🎬 February-April 1965: The making of the movie and album "HELP!"
  • 🎖 June 1965: Awarded 'Members of the British Empire' (MBE) by Queen Elizabeth
  • 🗽 August 1965: U.S. Tour (including 1st Stadium Concert by a rock band at Shea Stadum, NYC)
  • 🎤 October 1965: Recording the "Rubber Soul" Album
  • 🎤 April-June 1966: Recording the "Revolver" Album
  • 🌎 June-August 1966: World Tour
  • ⛱ September-October 1966: Hiatus and individual projects
  • 🎤November-December 1966: Recording demos for the "Sgt Pepper" Album
